The Wheel of Time

Set in a high fantasy world where magic exists, but only some can access it, a woman named Moiraine crosses path with five young men and women. This sparks a dangerous, world spanning journey. Based on the book series by Robert Jordan.

Last of us

The Last of Us follows the journey of Joel and Ellie across a post-apocalyptic America ravaged by a fungal infection that turns humans into aggressive creatures. Their initial delivery mission evolves into a profound bond as they face both infected threats and dangerous survivor groups while searching for a possible cure.

Lost Lands

"Lost Lands" is a fantasy adventure film following archaeologist Dr. Elena Marsh and her colleague as they're transported to a parallel dimension filled with prehistoric creatures and ancient technology. Their journey through this breathtaking world becomes a race against time as they battle a power-hungry general while searching for a way home.

Jujutsu Kaisen is a dark fantasy anime and manga series following Yuji Itadori, a high school student who becomes the vessel for Ryomen Sukuna, the King of Curses, after consuming a cursed finger. Under the guidance of powerful sorcerer Satoru Gojo at Tokyo Jujutsu High School, Yuji and his classmates battle dangerous cursed spirits while mastering their abilities to harness cursed energy.
